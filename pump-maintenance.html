Data-Driven Decision Support for Water Pump Maintenance

> University of Nottingham, Feb 2023 - Jun 2023
Machine Learning Statistics Python Exploratory Data Analysis Data Science

Goal

Develop an end-to-end pipeline to predict water pump failures using geographic, technical, and poor-quality data, addressing real-world maintenance challenges in developing regions.

Approach

  • Deep exploration and preprocessing of the dataset to handle poor-quality data
  • Feature engineering and dimensionality reduction techniques
  • Application of state-of-the-art classification models to optimize prediction quality
  • Collaborative development leveraging complementary strengths

Results

  • Prediction results superior to 96% of DrivenData competition entrants
  • Successful end-to-end solution for pump failure prediction
  • Enhanced teamwork and data science skills
