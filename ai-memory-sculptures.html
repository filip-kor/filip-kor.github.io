AI Memory Sculptures

> Creating Accessible Memory Preservation Method with Sentiment Analysis and Autonomous 3D Modelling

Goal

Create an innovative haptic alternative to photographs for memory preservation, making it accessible for visually impaired people through autonomous 3D modeling and emotion recognition.

Approach

  • Implementation of autonomous 3D modeling system
  • Emotion recognition from audio recordings and natural language
  • Integration of interdisciplinary research with industry sponsorship
  • Use of Python, Flask, CadQuery, and IBM APIs

Impact

Developed a new method for preserving memories through tactile 3D models, particularly beneficial for those unable to experience visual photographs due to impairments.

Skills: Interdisciplinary Research · Python (Programming Language) · Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) · Computer-Aided Design (CAD)
