Automatic Speech Recognition Model Compression

> ML Research Internship at Samsung R&D, Warsaw, Poland, June – September 2022
PyTorch Hugging Face Docker AWS Kubeflow Ubuntu

Overview

Focused on optimizing state-of-the-art Automatic Speech Recognition models for deployment in resource-constrained environments, particularly mobile and edge devices.

Technical Approach

  • Analysed and fine-tuned a Hugging Face transformer model using PyTorch, achieving significant performance improvements
  • Implemented model compression techniques including quantization and pruning to reduce model size by up to 70%
  • Explored cloud infrastructure with AWS, Docker containers, and Kubeflow pipelines for scalable training and deployment
  • Conducted extensive benchmarking on Ubuntu-based systems to validate compression vs. accuracy trade-offs

Key Challenges & Solutions

  • Balanced model compression with maintaining ASR accuracy above industry standards
  • Optimized training pipelines to reduce cloud costs while improving iteration speed
  • Collaborated with senior researchers to align technical work with business objectives

Results & Impact

  • Successfully compressed models while maintaining >95% of original accuracy
  • Demonstrated practical deployment feasibility on edge devices
  • Contributed to Samsung's voice technology research portfolio
